ABOUT MO

Hey there! I'm Mo, a tech enthusiast with a knack for blending creativity with code. My journey started back at the University of Michigan, where I tackled my undergrad in software engineering. But you know what really got my gears turning? Web development! I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and even jetted off to Germany for a cool course with Briannest Company.

Fast forward to seven years ago, and I found myself trading Persian palaces for American dreams, right here in the US of A. Yep, I made the leap to Ann Arbor, Michigan, where I'm currently a grad student at the University of Michigan, diving deep into the world of UX design and research. It's all about understanding how users interact with technology and crafting seamless experiences that make their lives easier.
