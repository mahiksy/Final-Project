INTELIGENCE

These heroes rely on magical abilities to control fights and deal damage. They have high mana pools and regeneration, making them effective spellcasters. They excel at disabling enemies and supporting allies with buffs and utility.

AGILITY

Agile and precise, these heroes deal physical damage with high attack speed. They carry their team in the late game, scaling well with items. They are mobile and elusive, capable of picking off targets and escaping danger.

STRENGTH

Sturdy and durable, these heroes have high health and armor. They initiate fights and soak up damage, creating space for their team. They often have crowd control abilities to disrupt enemies.

UNIVERSAL

Versatile and balanced, these heroes can adapt to various roles. They have a mix of attributes and abilities from intelligence, agility, and strength heroes. They provide flexibility and strategic depth to team compositions.
